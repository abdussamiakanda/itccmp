Md Faiyaz Bin Hassan is currently pursuing Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, USA. He is also working as a Graduate Research Assistant in Applied Electromagnetics Lab. His primary objective is to study electromagnetic behaviour of different materials. He graduated from the department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering in Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Sylhet, Bangladesh. He studied surface plasmon resonance phenomenon in photonic crystals for his undergraduate thesis.
