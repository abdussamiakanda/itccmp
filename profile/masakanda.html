Md Abdus Sami Akanda is a graduate student of Physics at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. His research focuses on the fascinating field of spintronics, exploring how the spin of electrons can be used to create innovative electronic devices. As an aspiring physicist, he is passionate about teaching others and sharing his love for physics.

Research Interests

  • Spintronics
  • Magnetization/Domain Wall Dynamics
  • Spin Waves
  • Magnonic Crystal
