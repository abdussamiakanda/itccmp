This course serves as a comprehensive introduction to the foundations of classical mechanics, from the modern point of view of 21st-century theoretical physics. It develops the intuition of the students focusing on both physical and mathematical formalism of classical phenomena. It is focused more on Lagrangian mechanics than Newtonian mechanics.

Lectures of this course will be given every week, from 10:00 pm to 11:30 pm starting from 27 January 2022. The course is taught online under Fermion Physics Club via Google Meet.
The live lectures are recorded. Registered students will receive information about the class times via emails.
