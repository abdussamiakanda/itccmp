Everything around us is driven by electricity and magnetism. The universe exists because of electricity and magnetism. This course serves as a comprehensive introduction to the foundations of electricity and magnetism. It develops the intuition of the students focusing on both physical and mathematical formalism of electric and magnetic phenomena. This course focused more on physics than mathematics.

Lectures of this course were given every Friday from 8:00 pm to 9:30 pm, starting from April 2020 to May 2020. The course was taught online under Fermion Physics Club via Zoom, due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
The live lectures were recorded and uploaded to YouTube. Students received information about how to attend the classes via messenger group.
