Key Features and Benefits:

  • Processor: Equipped with an AMD Ryzen 7 7800X3D 4.2 GHz 8-Core Processor, providing exceptional performance and multitasking capabilities.
  • Memory: Boasting G.Skill Trident Z5 RGB 64 GB (2 x 32 GB) DDR5-6400 CL32 Memory, ensuring smooth and efficient operation even with the most demanding applications and simulations.
  • Graphics: Featuring a Gigabyte WINDFORCE OC GeForce RTX 4070 GPU with 12GB of GDDR6X memory, delivering unparalleled graphics performance for data processing and visualization tasks.
  • Storage: With a lightning-fast Samsung 980 Pro 2 TB M.2-2280 PCIe 4.0 X4 NVME Solid State Drive, accessing and storing large datasets and files is quick and seamless.
  • Remote Access: The PC serves as a remote computational server, providing our team with access to powerful computing resources from anywhere, at any time, facilitating collaboration and productivity.
  • Scalability: Designed with scalability in mind, the PC offers ample room for future expansion and upgrades, ensuring that our computational infrastructure remains at the forefront of technological innovation.
