Welcome to the Institution for Theoretical and Computational Physics Research

Our Story


In December 2023, we, a small group of graduate students from Bangladesh studying in the USA, came together with a shared dream. That dream was the Institution for Theoretical and Computational Physics Research (ITCPR), born out of our collective passion for physics and a desire to make research opportunities more accessible.

Our Vision


At ITCPR, our vision is to be a pioneering force in the realm of theoretical and computational physics, breaking new ground in research and innovation. We aspire to create a global community where passion for physics knows no boundaries, and opportunities for groundbreaking research are accessible to everyone, regardless of their geographical or socioeconomic background. Our goal is to equip aspiring physicists from underrepresented regions with the tools, knowledge, and platform they need to excel and contribute to the scientific world. Through collaboration, mentorship, and a commitment to excellence, we aim to not only advance the field of physics but also inspire a new generation of scientists who will continue to push the frontiers of what is possible.

Our Objectives


Building a Global Research Community
  • Establishing a network for aspiring physicists, particularly from regions with limited research opportunities.
  • Promoting collaboration and exchange of ideas across different cultures and backgrounds.
Advancing Theoretical and Computational Physics
  • Engaging in cutting-edge research and exploration in key areas of theoretical and computational physics.
  • Encouraging innovative approaches and problem-solving techniques in physics research.
Providing Comprehensive Training and Education
  • Offering in-depth training programs and educational resources in theoretical and computational physics.
  • Organizing online workshops, seminars, and lectures to enhance learning and skill development.
Developing Digital and Research Skills
  • Equipping members with essential digital skills, including programming, data analysis, and use of scientific software.
  • Fostering research methodology skills, critical for academic and professional growth in physics.
Promoting Science Communication and Outreach
  • Creating platforms for members to share research findings and insights, such as blogs or digital magazines.
  • Engaging with the wider community to popularize physics and scientific inquiry.

These objectives aim to position ITCPR as a leading institution in theoretical and computational physics research, bridging gaps in opportunity and fostering a diverse, skilled, and interconnected global community of physicists.
