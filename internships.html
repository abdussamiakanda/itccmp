Internship Programs


Join our vibrant team as an intern and immerse yourself in cutting-edge research in theoretical and computational physics. Our internship programs offer unique opportunities to work alongside motivated scientists and contribute to significant projects.

Program Overview


Our internships provide hands-on experience in various aspects of physics research. You'll gain practical skills, work on real-world problems, and be part of a team pushing the boundaries of scientific knowledge.

Eligibility Criteria


  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a relevant undergraduate or graduate program.
  • A strong academic record and a keen interest in theoretical and computational physics.
  • Ability to commit to the full duration of the internship.

Application Process


To apply for an internship, please submit the following:

  • A detailed resume or CV highlighting relevant education and experience.
  • A cover letter expressing your interest in the internship and what you hope to gain from the experience.
  • Any additional materials, such as academic transcripts or letters of recommendation, if required.

Submit your application to internships@itcpr.org.
