Internship Programs
Dive into our vibrant internship programs at ITCPR, where you'll have the unique opportunity to immerse yourself in cutting-edge research in theoretical and computational physics. Our program begins with a focused three-month training period, during which interns will gain foundational knowledge in the field and develop essential skills. Following this training, you'll transition into hands-on projects, joining a team of motivated scientists to tackle real-world problems and contribute significantly to advancing scientific knowledge.
Eligibility Criteria
  • Open to students currently enrolled in an undergraduate program (minimum 2nd year) or a graduate program in physics or a related field.
  • Demonstrated interest in the specific research fields offered at ITCPR. For more information on our research areas, visit our Research Groups page.
  • Commitment to engage fully for the entire duration of the internship program.
  • Agree to adhere to to the institutional policies of ITCPR.
Application Process
To apply for an internship at ITCPR, kindly prepare the following materials:
  • A comprehensive CV detailing your educational background and any relevant experiences.
  • A cover letter that clearly expresses your enthusiasm for participating in our internship program, highlights the particular research group you wish to join, and defines your goals and expectations for the internship experience.
  • Verification documents for student status, such as academic transcripts or grade sheets. Note: These documents are for verification purposes only and will not influence the acceptance decision.
Once you have prepared these materials, proceed to the ITCPR Portal to submit your application. The portal will guide you through the application process, allowing you to submit the required documents for review.
Application Deadlines
Our internship programs are meticulously organized into one key recruitment cycle annually, synchronized with our operational approach to guarantee seamless integration of interns into our projects and collaborative efforts. The application portal for internships opens on the 1st of December and closes on the 20th of December each year. We emphasize the importance of adhering to the specified application timelines to facilitate a smooth and efficient entry into our programs, as we strictly observe the window for application submissions.
What to Expect
Upon selection, interns will be notified via email. They may also be called for an interview as part of the final evaluation process. Once confirmed, they will join the dynamic environment of ITCPR, actively engaging in our research initiatives and contributing to the vibrant culture of scientific exploration and discovery within our projects.
Contact Information
For more information or inquiries about our internship programs, contact us at info@itcpr.org.
